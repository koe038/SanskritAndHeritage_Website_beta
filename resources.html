Resources to learn Sanskrit


If you want to learn Sanskrit and introduce yourself to the rich heritage woven by the ancient speakers of this language, then you might find the following resources useful. Bon voyage!

Basic Spoken Sanskrit: This is the best place to start out if you want to learn Sanskrit from scratch. You will start from the basics and slowly build up on grammar and your vocabulary, and in the end, you can successfully hold a basic conversation with someone else in the language. Ready to dive in? Go on and check out the playlist. Here, you can find the corresponding lecture slides.

Sanskrit Dictionary: A strong vocabulary is a necessity when it comes to learning a language properly, without which, it gets really difficult to express your thoughts clearly. This site is a Sanskrit-English (and vice-versa) dictionary. Head over to it to learn nrew words and get a stronger grasp of the language. you might also find the following websites useful: Vyomlabs, Openpathshala.

A Handful of Sanskrit Tools: This site has several onlne and offline tools for those who wish to learn Sanskrit digitally. Whether you want to learn grammar or wish to access a dictionary, head over here o find the same.

Sanskrit Sandhi Rules: Here's a great site if you wish to learn the rules of Sandhi.

Intermediate Level of Spoken Sanskrit: So you've mastered the basics of Sanskrit and have overcome the challenges of acquiring fluency with all the vibhaktis and would like to advance further? This playlist is there to guide you along.
