Bharata Tirtha: An International Webinar on IKS at IIT Kharagpur




IIT Kharagpur, Nov. 6th-8th, 2020: This three day webinar on Indian Knowledge Systems at IIT Kharagpur was inaugurated virtually by Chief Guest Dr. Ramesh Pokriyal, Union Minsiter of Education on 6th November, 2020. The aim of the webinar was to trace the ancient philosophical and scientific heritage of India from the core of the srutis; the timeless oracular tradition of Indian spirituality and Yoga; from the heart and essence of the sutras of the Seven Supreme Sages, the “Saptarishis”; from where all later Episteme of all Indian knowledge systems have been derived. It was a part of a long series of subsequent ones, and the aim is to advance the lines of thought and action plans on our thinking on the future of Indology and its application to the contemporary human world, and solve the manifold plethora of crisis in the physical, economic, environmental, and social sphere of human life in relationship to the Cosmos.

The seminar witnessed talks by international experts from the best institutes around the world in the domains of Arthashastra (Economics), Sanskrit for NLP, Numeral System, Bijaganita and Jyamiti (Vedic and Anscient Indian Mathematics) Ayurveda (Biological Sciences), Rasayana (Chemical Sciences), Jyotir-tatha Mahajagatika Vidya (Positional and Astronomical Sciences), Prakriti Vidya (Terrestrial/ Material Sciences/ Ecology and Atmospheric Sciences), Nandana Tathya tatha Vastu Vidya (Archaeology, Iconography and Architecture) etc.

As announced by Sri Ramesh Pokhriyal, IIT Kharagour is to set up a Center of Excellence for Indian Knowledge Systems. You can read more about that here.
