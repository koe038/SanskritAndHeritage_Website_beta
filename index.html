ABOUT THE WEBSITE

Swāgatam! Sanskrit, or Sanskritam (meaning well-done), is one of the oldest languages, belonging to the Indo-Aryan branch of the Indo-European family of languages. This language is a crucial part of our identities as Bharatiyas, not only is it an important aspect of Hindu tradition, but also the cardinal key to the vault containing indespensible treasures from the past, the key to understanding fascinating works on philosophy, science, law, spirituality, consciousness and beyond. The rich heritage of our land, interwoven firmly with Sanskrit, is a gift from our ancestors that we should cherish... Unfortunately, in the present day, Sanskrit is not essentially "widely spoken". Unlike the other languages which branched out from it and retained only the communicative characteristics, Sanskrit is a language that allows one to express true experience. When studied consciously, the language directly enhances the functioning of the mind and the body, instils healthy curiosity and an open and eager mind that welcomes knowledge, and also brings about a sense of peace, clarity and happiness in the learner. The systematic structure of the language also enables rational thinking, and on the whole, Sanskrit is capable of bringing about a great transformation.

As we embark on a journey as samayayātrikāḥ trying to unlock the vault of our mysterious past, we would like to invite you to join us, in hope that together, we can unveil these secrets and the plethora of knowledge that is buried deep down in time and space.
